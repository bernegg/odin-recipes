Cornbread Muffin Recipe

Description

Looking for a perfect Southern side dish to pair with all your favorite comfort foods? Corn muffins are always a good idea. This top-rated corn muffin recipe is incredibly easy to make, comes together quickly with pantry staples, and will be beloved by everyone at your table.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cornmeal
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/3 cup white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1/4 cup canola oil
  • 1 cup milk

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Grease muffin pan or line with paper muffin liners.
  2. In a large bowl, mix together corn meal, flour, sugar, baking powder and salt.
  3. Add egg, oil and milk; stir gently to combine.
  4. Spoon batter into prepared muffin cups.
  5. Bake at 400 degrees F (200 degrees C) for 15 to 20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into a muffin comes out clean.
  6. Enjoy!
